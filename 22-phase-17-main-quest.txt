17.01 — ACT 1: WAKING (Crypt → Black Harbor)
  17.01.001 — Stage 1.1: "Wake in the crypt"
    - Trigger: new_game
    - Text: "You wake. Stone. Cold. Breath like smoke. Your hands are clasped around a blade that hums."
    - Cutscene: 5s fade-in, ambient audio
    - Action: spawn player at sc_coast_01 (30,20)
  17.01.002 — Stage 1.2: "Examine the blade"
    - Trigger: examine entity blade_moving_tongue
    - Text: "The inscriptions move. The blade remembers what its bearer forgets."
    - Action: pickup blade, set flag blade_acquired, language_understand=25, start tutorial "moving language"
    - Cinematic: blade glows, inscriptions animate 3s
  17.01.003 — Stage 1.3: "Find the exit"
    - Trigger: open crypt door (Crypt Key not needed yet, blade opens it)
    - Text: "The crypt door yields to the blade as if it remembers you."
    - Action: open stone_door sc_coast_01 → sc_coast_02
    - Sound: stone_grind.wav
  17.01.004 — Stage 1.4: "Reach the surface"
    - Trigger: enter sc_coast_02
    - Text: "The wind hits you first. Then the salt. Then the endless grey."
    - Cinematic: view of coast, ambient sea_wind.wav swells
  17.01.005 — Stage 1.5: "Find the living"
    - Trigger: enter sc_coast_03 (Saltmarket)
    - Text: "A market. Voices. The smell of fish and smoke. People look at you strangely."
    - Cinematic: 3s pan across market
  17.01.006 — Stage 1.6: "Speak with the Watch"
    - Trigger: enter Watchtower or speak to Iola
    - Branches:
      - 1.6a (Watch rep >= 0): Iola offers recruitment
      - 1.6b (Watch rep < 0): Iola suspicious, harder path
      - 1.6c (player declined earlier): different dialogue
  17.01.007 — Stage 1.7 BRANCH: "Join the Watch / Walk alone"
    - Choice: join_coastal_watch vs decline
    - If join: rep +5 Watch, main_act2_join_cartographers, fast-track
    - If decline: more freedom, slower progression, different Act 2 opener
  17.01.008 — Stage 1.8: "The blade whispers"
    - Trigger: language_understand reaches 30
    - Text: "First word on the blade: 'covenant'. The letters move like they want to speak."
    - Tutorial: moving language basics
  17.01.009 — Stage 1.9: "Old Maren sees the blade"
    - Trigger: speak to Maren with blade equipped
    - Text: "She sees the blade and her face goes pale. 'You carry the moving tongue. Where did you find it? More importantly — where did it find you?'"
    - Quest hook: visit lighthouse
  17.01.010 — Stage 1.10: "Lighthouse vision"
    - Trigger: top of sc_coast_09 at dawn (6am)
    - Text: "The light catches a shard above the horizon. It pulses. Once. Twice. Then darkness."
    - Vision: floating shard visible for 3s
    - Action: set flag shard_seen_1, start main_act1_first_shard
  17.01.011 — Stage 1.11: "Travel to Black Harbor"
    - Trigger: arrive sc_coast_12
    - Cinematic: 5s descent into dark harbor
  17.01.012 — Stage 1.12: "Confront the Drowned King"
    - Trigger: enter throne room
    - Boss fight: The Drowned King
    - 3 phases: tide rises, summons adds, final stand
  17.01.013 — Stage 1.13 BRANCH: "Spare / Kill / Banish"
    - Choice: 3 options
    - 1.13a (spare): +5 Choir, -2 Watch, "He deserved better."
    - 1.13b (kill): +5 Watch, -2 Choir, "Justice. Or vengeance. Hard to say."
    - 1.13c (banish): neutral, secret reward "covenant_fragment_1"
  17.01.014 — Stage 1.14: "Receive the shard"
    - Action: player gains shard_1_first (key quest item)
    - Text: "The shard is cold. It hums when you touch it. The blade seems to recognize it."
    - Reward: XP 2000, items
  17.01.015 — Stage 1.15: "Choose your path"
    - Trigger: flag shard_1_acquired
    - Opens Act 2 selection: Cartographers (Glasswood), Sky-Smiths (Spire), Choir (Hollow Cities), Free
    - Branches affect Act 2 opening dialogue
