ASH — Phase 08: NPC Life
===========================

Phase goal: NPCs with daily schedules, dialogue trees (DSL parser
and runtime), disposition rules, faction reputation.

Entry criteria: Phase 7 complete.
Exit criteria: 5 NPCs in a test town have working schedules,
              dialogue trees, and disposition; talking to them
              at different times of day yields different dialogue.

[ENTRY CRITERIA]
  - Phase 7 complete.

[EXIT CRITERIA]
  - NPC schedules parsed and executed.
  - Dialogue DSL parser works.
  - Dialogue UI shows topics and skill checks.
  - Disposition affects dialogue.
  - Faction reputation tracked.

[RISKS]
  - DSL parser bugs. Mitigation: comprehensive tests, error
    reporting at load.
  - Schedule complexity. Mitigation: visual debugger (later).
  - Dialogue runtime bugs. Mitigation: trace mode for dev.
