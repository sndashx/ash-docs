ASH — Phase 06: Character System
==================================

Phase goal: 9 attributes, 24 skills, leveling, derived stats,
inventory, equipment, conditions.

Entry criteria: Phase 5 complete.
Exit criteria: character UI shows all stats; leveling works;
              inventory manages items; equipment affects stats.

[ENTRY CRITERIA]
  - Phase 5 complete.

[EXIT CRITERIA]
  - Attributes and skills editable via character UI.
  - XP and leveling functional.
  - Inventory add/remove/drop/equip.
  - Equipped items modify derived stats.
  - Conditions apply effects.

[RISKS]
  - Derived stat circular dependencies. Mitigation: explicit
    recompute order.
  - Inventory edge cases. Mitigation: comprehensive tests.
