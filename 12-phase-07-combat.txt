ASH — Phase 07: Combat
=========================

Phase goal: real-time-with-pause combat. Weapons, armor, status
effects, AI behaviors, pathfinding, line-of-sight, cover.

Entry criteria: Phase 6 complete.
Exit criteria: combat encounter with 3 bandits works; player can
              fight, use items, flee, win, loot corpses.

[ENTRY CRITERIA]
  - Phase 6 complete.

[EXIT CRITERIA]
  - RTwP combat loop functional.
  - All weapon types work.
  - Armor damage reduction functional.
  - Status effects apply and tick.
  - AI uses pathfinding and LoS.
  - Player can die (and reload).

[RISKS]
  - Combat balance. Mitigation: data-driven, easy to tune.
  - AI decision-making complexity. Mitigation: composable behaviors.
  - Performance with many combatants. Mitigation: LOD updates.
