PHASE 12 — SCOURED COAST (REGION 1)
=====================================
Region: Coastal starting area, salt-blasted cliffs, half-sunken cities, smuggler holds.
Maps: 12 (sc_coast_01 through sc_coast_12)
Faction primary: Coastal Watch (steel blue)
Key NPCs: Captain Iola Vesh, Old Maren the Salt-Reader, Pell the Lock-Keeper, Brother Wyan
Theme: Memory and tides. The waking begins here.
